# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Keep interactions respectful, factual, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive when giving feedback.
- Assume good intent unless evidence suggests otherwise.
- Keep review comments tied to behavior, tests, documentation, or maintainability.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate misinformation in reviews or documentation.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.

## Enforcement

Issues that violate this standard should be handled through the repository's normal private or maintainers-only communication path.
